📋 Key Policies
👤 New Patient Policy
  • Not seen in 1+ year = treated as new patient
  • Must arrive 15–20 minutes early for paperwork
  • Collect: Insurance · DOB · Name · Phone · Email · Address
Out of Pocket: $250 for new patient visit
Out of Pocket: $130 for follow up visit
📅 Cancellation Policy
Within 24 hours: Do NOT cancel or mark no-show. Add note: "Pt requested to cancel (reason) – rescheduled to [date/time]" and save appointment.

24+ hours prior: Cancel without notes.
💵 No-Show Fees
No fee: Dr. Islam · Dr. Murad · Michelle White

All others charge:
New patient no-show: $50
Follow-up no-show: $25

🖥️ E-Visit Policy
We do not schedule e-visits from the scheduling line. These are handled by the front desk at each location. Transfer the call to the IVR and guide patient to press the correct option for their location.
📟 Voicemail Instructions
Instruct patients to clearly state:
  • First and last name
  • Date of birth
  • Their concern / reason for call
  • Best callback number
⚠️ HMO Insurance Reminder
All HMO insurance policies require a referral from the PCP before scheduling. Verify this when scheduling new patients or when a follow-up patient updates their insurance.
🩺 Provider Role Definitions
MD — Medical Director
Diagnoses complex mental illness, prescribes medications, manages related medical conditions. Highest level of medical training.
NP — Nurse Practitioner
Treats mental illness holistically with some prescribing ability. Less medical training than MD but full scope of psychiatric care.
Therapist / Counselor
Provides counseling and talk therapy only. Cannot prescribe medications. Focuses on behavioral and emotional support.
